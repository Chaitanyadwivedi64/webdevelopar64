Ganesh Chaturthi

Ganesh Chaturthi, also known as Vinayaka Chaturthi, is a major Hindu festival celebrating the birth of Lord Ganesha, the remover of obstacles and the god of wisdom and prosperity.

About the Festival

The festival is observed on the fourth day (Chaturthi) of the Shukla Paksha in the Bhadrapada month of the Hindu calendar, which usually falls in August or September. In 2025, Ganesh Chaturthi will be celebrated on August 27.

Traditions and Rituals

  • Installation of Ganesha idols at homes and public pandals
  • Pranapratishtha – ritual to invoke the deity into the idol
  • Daily prayers, aartis, and offerings of sweets like modak
  • Cultural events, music, and dance performances
  • Visarjan – immersion of the idol in water on the 10th day

Significance

Ganesh Chaturthi symbolizes new beginnings, wisdom, and the removal of obstacles. It brings communities together in celebration and devotion.
